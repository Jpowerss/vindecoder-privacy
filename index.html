Privacy Policy

VIN Verify is built so the developer never sees your data — because the developer has no servers to store it on.

Last Updated: April 2026

In Plain English

VIN Verify is a vehicle information lookup tool. The developer has no servers and collects no data from this app. Your garage, inspections, maintenance records, and photos stay on your device and in your own iCloud private database. Vehicle lookups go directly from your device to public third-party services that you choose to query. No tracking, no analytics, no advertising, no third-party SDKs.

1What We Do Not Do

  • We do not collect any data from this app
  • We do not operate any servers that receive your data
  • We do not use any analytics — no Firebase, Google Analytics, Mixpanel, or similar
  • We do not use any crash-reporting SDKs — no Crashlytics, Sentry, or similar
  • We do not display any advertising or use ad networks
  • We do not track you across apps or websites
  • We do not share or sell any data to third parties — we have no data to share or sell
  • We do not require an account to use the app

2What Stays on Your Device

The following data is stored on your device. When you are signed into iCloud, it syncs to your own iCloud private database, which only you can access:

  • Your saved garage of vehicles
  • Inspection records and inspection photos
  • Maintenance records and audit reports
  • Vehicle health items and reminders
  • App preferences and settings
Sync uses Apple's CloudKit framework. The developer cannot access data in your iCloud private database — it is stored under your control. For details on how Apple handles iCloud data, see Apple's Privacy Policy.

3External Lookups Initiated by You

When you use features in the app, your device may make HTTPS requests directly to third-party services for public vehicle data. These requests do not pass through any server the developer operates.

Feature Data sent to the third-party service
VIN decode The VIN you entered
Recall lookup Year, make, model
Crash test ratings Year, make, model
Owner complaints Year, make, model
Fuel economy Year, make, model
Live gas prices None — fetches public national-average data
EV charging stations Your approximate location, only when you choose to look up nearby chargers
Vehicle thumbnail images Year, make, model

These services are operated by independent third parties — including public agencies and public-data registries — under their own privacy policies. The developer has no access to or control over how those third parties handle requests they receive.

4Permissions the App Requests

Permission Why When asked
Camera To scan VIN barcodes or text from a vehicle Only when you tap a "scan" button
Photo Library To scan a VIN from a saved image, attach photos to vehicles in your garage, and add a custom logo to PDF reports Only when you initiate one of those features
Location When In Use To find EV charging stations near you for electric vehicles in your garage Only when you view an electric vehicle's detail page and use the charging stations feature

Camera and photo data are processed entirely on-device. Image data never leaves your device.

5Purchases

In-app purchases (subscriptions and one-time purchases) are handled entirely by Apple's StoreKit framework. The developer receives only an entitlement signal — whether you have an active subscription or have completed a one-time purchase. The developer does not see your name, email, payment information, or any other Apple ID detail.

Apple's privacy policy applies to purchase data: apple.com/legal/privacy

6Children's Privacy

The app is not directed at children under 13. The developer does not knowingly receive any data from anyone, adult or minor.

7Changes to This Policy

This policy may be updated periodically to reflect new features or changes to data handling. The "Last Updated" date at the top of this page reflects the most recent revision. Material changes will be noted in the App Store release notes for the next version.
