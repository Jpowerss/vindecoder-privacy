3External Lookups Initiated by You

When you use features in the app, your device may make HTTPS requests directly to third-party services for public vehicle data. These requests do not pass through any server the developer operates.

Feature Data sent to the third-party service
VIN decode The VIN you entered
Recall lookup Year, make, model
Crash test ratings Year, make, model
Owner complaints Year, make, model
Fuel economy Year, make, model
Live gas prices None — fetches public national-average data
EV charging stations Your approximate location, only when you choose to look up nearby chargers
Vehicle thumbnail images Year, make, model

These services are operated by independent third parties — including public agencies and public-data registries — under their own privacy policies. The developer has no access to or control over how those third parties handle requests they receive.
